Shonchester United

Also Known As: Asia Hall F.C (2022), Renaissance F.C (2023)

Founded in 2021 as Asia Hall F.C, the team only composed of Nic, Eddie, a few suitemates from the eponymous Asia Hall Residence Halls in ERC, and a few randoms recruited from IMLeague's free agents (Daniel, Eric, and Mark).

After a disastrous winless first season, we rebranded to Renaissance F.C to reflect our new living conditions, and saw a marked improvement in results along with a slew of new players, notably Allen Y. at GK, Allen K. at MF, and Mo at centerback. Though the team wasn't able to advance past the first round of playoffs, we were able to witness the legendary Shon John RKO against Hermanos F.C. We would end the season going 2-1-3.

Season 3 would see us rebrand to Shonchester United in honor of Shon's violent demeanor and blatant corruption, and we would have our most successful season of all time, finishing the season undefeated in normal time. With the introduction of Robbie and Pato, we would dominate the latter half of the season though eventually we would lose a heartbreaking penalty shootout against the eventual champions, KAN F.C.

Our final campaign would see us continue our strong performances, finishing 3rd in the league and advancing to the semifinals of the playoffs once more, though we would lose to Dos Santos UTD in normal time.

Overall, we would finish our four years with a record of 13 wins, 5 draws, and 7 losses, with a total of 34 goals scored and 35 goals conceded, with 46 different players representing Shonchester United over the years.
